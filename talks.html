Talks & Presentations

Selected talks, presentations, and panel discussions on AI governance, ethics, and public policy.

October 2025

The 'Wild West' of Medicine: Exploring the Emergence of ‘Grassroots’ AI Governance in Radiology.

2025 AAAI/ACM Conference on AI, Ethics, and Society Madrid, Spain

Offered a presentation slot (offered to less than 10% of accepted papers) at the 2025 AIES conference for recent publication exploring AI governance efforts emerging within hospitals in the US and UK.

March 2024

Reframing Governance as Innovation: Steamboat Accidents and their Lessons for AI Governance

Cornell University School of Information Science Seminar Series Online

Presented research comparing the current challenges posed by AI governance to those encountered while regulating steamboats in the 1800's.

Centre for Data Ethics and Innovation Teach-In Series London, UK
